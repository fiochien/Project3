MEET THE BREADS

Bread Buddies was created to teach kids about embracing their creativity and developing life skills through making delicious bread! Each Bread Buddy is given a unique character that your child will befriend on their journey to becoming an amazing baker.

Our lessons include basic bread, Japanese milk bread, pull-apart garlic bread, focaccia, na'an, and chocolate croissants!

Sign up today to see how your child can make some Bread Buddies!
