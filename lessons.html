LESSON 1 :

BASIC BREAD

When you think of bread, it is like a delicious, soft, and squishy cushion for your favorite sandwich fillings. It is one of the most popular foods around the world, and it's made from just a few simple ingredients!

LESSON 2 :

JAPANESE MILK BREAD

Japanese milk bread, often known as "shokupan" in Japan, is a special kind of bread that's super soft and fluffy, almost like a cloud in your mouth. It's called "milk bread" because it's made with milk, which gives it a slightly sweet and creamy flavor.

LESSON 3 :

PULL-APART GARLIC BREAD

Pull-apart garlic bread is a yummy and fun way to enjoy garlic bread with your friends and family. It's called "pull-apart" because you can easily tear it into pieces, almost like you're playing with your food.

LESSON 4 :

FOCACCIA

Focaccia is a type of bread that's kind of like a mix between pizza and regular bread. It's a bit thicker and softer than regular bread, and it's often flavored with yummy toppings. If you love pizza, this is perfect for you!

LESSON 5 :

NA'AN

Naan is a type of bread that's popular in many parts of the world, especially in countries like India and Pakistan. It's quite different from the bread you might be used to, like sliced bread or rolls.

LESSON 6 :

CHOCOLATE CROISSANT

Chocolate croissants, also known as "chocolate croissants" or "pain au chocolat" in French, are like a delightful treat that's a mix of flaky, buttery pastry and sweet chocolate. This is great for those with a sweet tooth.
