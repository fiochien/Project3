LESSON 1 :

BASIC BREAD

When you think of bread, it is like a delicious, soft, and squishy cushion for your favorite sandwich fillings. It is one of the most popular foods around the world, and it's made from just a few simple ingredients!

1 hr 5 min

INGREDIENTS

  • - 3 cups all purpose flour
  • - 1 tsp salt
  • - ½ tsp active dry yeast
  • - 1¾ cups warm water

*additional flour needed for dusting the countertop and bread

EQUIPMENT

  • - mixing bowls
  • - measuring cups
  • - measuring spoons
  • - whisk, spatula, or spoon
  • - oval casserole dish

BAKING START !

1

In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and yeast. Mix with a spatula, whisk, or clean hands. Add the water and mix until you have a sticky dough.

2

Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let rest at room temperature for 5 hours or overnight. Place the bowl of dough away from drafty areas, such as in the microwave or in a cold oven.

3

After the dough has tripled in size, uncover it, and flour the counter. Pour the dough out onto the counter and generously sprinkle the top with flour.

4

You do NOT need to knead the dough. You can simply move it around until you have a really nice and soft dough that is no longer sticky and is oval in shape. If the loaf sticks to your hands or the counter, sprinkle on a little more flour.

5

Place the loaf into any oven-safe dish lined with parchment paper. Place a lid on the dish, and place the dish in a cold, NOT-PREHEATED oven.

6

Turn the oven on to 450F (230C) once the bread is inside. Bake the bread with the lid on the dish for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, carefully remove the hot lid from the dish and bake the bread for another 25 to 30 minutes.

7

The bake time will depend on your oven, so keep an eye on it. Total bake time will be about 1 hour from the moment you place the bread into the cold oven.

8

The bread is done once it’s light and golden all over. It is still hot so let it cool for 5 - 10 minutes! Once your bread has cooled off, it is time to dig in. Enjoy!
